OUR FOUNDER
OUR FOUNDER

As a Montessori, we have successfully nurtured young minds, fostering independence, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning in students and proud of what they've become today !

Founded on December 25, 2008, Sirithibas Montessori was established through the vision and dedication of Mrs. Mallika Wijewickrama, a resident of South Carolina, USA. Inspired by the advice of Venerable Pelmadulle Dhammika Nahimi during his stay in the USA, Mrs. Wijewickrama built the preschool to honor her beloved parents, Mr. De Samaraweera and Mrs. Padma Samaraweera. Venerable Pelmadulle Dhammika Nahimi, the founder and director, serves as the Deputy Chief Judicial Sanghanayaka Shastravedi of Sabaragamu Province and is associated with Sri Gnanarama Purana Viharaya and Sri Daluggala Sri Maitri Bodhi Temple. His esteemed background and commitment to education and peace have guided the Montessori's mission to nurture and develop young minds with the values of independence, creativity, and lifelong learning. We are truly proud of our Montessori's evolution. From our humble beginnings with only 23 students, we have grown to a thriving community of 150 students. As we look to the future, our vision is to expand Sirithibas Montessori into an advanced learning preschool and international school, offering education up to Grade 5. We are excited to continue our journey, fostering a love of learning and nurturing the potential of every child in our care.
Our Vision is to produce valueble students to world as a proud sri lanakan montessori. - Pellmadulle Dhammika Thero
    Founder & Director at Sirithibas Pre-school
